## Slide 2：Why Narrow Long-Video Benchmark to I2V?（1.75分钟）

### 中文讲稿

课题最初计划构建分钟级长视频生成Benchmark。调研后我发现，最关键的问题不是各模型的比较协议不同，而是很多所谓的长视频结果并非一次性联合生成完整分钟级序列，而是在短片段生成器上反复进行条件续写。

技术报告给出了直接证据。StreamingT2V把当时主流方法擅长的范围概括为最长约16秒，并指出不少模型主要生成16帧或24帧短序列；它通过前序片段特征和长期外观信息逐块扩展。ViD-GPT和Ca2-VDM更直接地写道，多数视频扩散模型采用自回归扩展，即让后续片段以此前片段的末帧或末若干帧为条件。STIV则给出一个很具体的实现：先用20帧模型生成片段，再把上一轮最后两帧作为下一轮的图像条件，连续滚动四次得到更长视频。

上述证据支持一个更准确的判断：许多长视频系统的核心操作，确实是“前一段末帧或少量前序帧，加提示词，生成下一段”。从条件结构看，每次滚动都接近一次I2V或少量帧到视频的局部状态转移。如果一次续写中发生身份漂移、动作绑错主体或相机运动混淆，错误还会被带入后续片段并逐步累积。

但我不会把它表述成“长视频等同于I2V”。FreeNoise采用滑动窗口，FIFO-Diffusion采用队列式去噪，NUWA-XL采用层次化关键帧生成，说明长视频还有其他技术路线。同时，LVD-2M和MovieBench表明专用长视频数据需要重新筛选和分层标注，Beyond FVD与SLVMEval也说明长时序自动评测器仍需专门验证。因此，本课题的调整是机制拆解，而不是概念替换：先把分段续写反复依赖的I2V局部能力做成可控、可归因的Benchmark，未来再把它嵌入完整的长视频系统级评测。

### English Script

The project originally aimed to build a benchmark for minute-level video generation. The literature review changed my view of the problem. The key issue is not simply that different models use different evaluation settings. Many reported long videos are produced through repeated short-horizon generation rather than a single, joint generation process.

The technical reports provide direct evidence. StreamingT2V describes the strong range of earlier systems as short clips of up to about sixteen seconds and extends them chunk by chunk. ViD-GPT and Ca2-VDM explicitly describe a common autoregressive setting in which each new clip is conditioned on the final frame or frames of the previous clip. STIV gives a concrete example: it uses the last two frames of one rollout as image conditions for the next rollout.

This means that an important family of long-video systems repeatedly solves a local image- or few-frame-conditioned generation problem. Errors in identity preservation, action binding, or camera-motion interpretation can therefore pass into later clips and accumulate over time.

However, long-video generation is not equivalent to repeated I2V. FreeNoise, FIFO-Diffusion, and NUWA-XL use window-based, queue-based, or hierarchical strategies. Long-video data and long-horizon evaluators also require substantial independent work. I therefore treat this change as mechanism decomposition, not task substitution: the current benchmark isolates a reusable local capability that can later become one component of a system-level long-video benchmark.

## Slide 3：I2V Motivation & Benchmark Gap（1.25分钟）

### 中文讲稿

为什么选择I2V组合能力作为切口？因为视觉质量并不等于指令遵循。页面中的两只狗是概念示例，不是实验结果：输入图像中有一只白狗和一只棕狗，指令要求“让白狗坐下”。模型可能让棕狗坐下，也可能只做镜头缩放。这些结果看起来仍然流畅，但主体和动作没有正确绑定。

现有工作提供了重要基础：T2I-CompBench不涉及时间动态；T2V-CompBench主要使用纯文本输入；VBench和VBench++覆盖广泛质量维度，但组合失败归因不是其唯一重点。

I2V的特殊性在于双重约束：输入图像定义了主体身份、初始布局和背景，文本只要求其中一部分变化。本研究因此把“必须变化的内容”和“必须保持的内容”组织为显式的Preserve–Transform结构，用于判断变化有没有发生、发生在谁身上，以及是否破坏了非目标内容。

### English Script

Why focus on compositional capability in I2V? Because visual quality and instruction following are not the same. Consider the conceptual example on this slide. The input contains a white dog and a brown dog, and the instruction asks the white dog to sit. A model may make the brown dog sit, or it may only zoom the camera. Both outputs can still look smooth, but neither applies the requested action to the correct subject.

Existing benchmarks provide strong foundations, but they target different questions. T2I-CompBench does not model temporal change. T2V-CompBench mainly uses text-only conditions. VBench and VBench++ cover a broad range of video qualities, while fine-grained attribution of compositional errors is not their sole focus.

I2V introduces two coupled constraints. The input image defines the initial subjects, layout, and background, while the text requests a selective change. I therefore represent each task with an explicit Preserve–Transform structure: what must change, what must remain stable, and which subject the change should affect.

---

## Slide 4：Preserve–Transform Framework（1.25分钟）

### 中文讲稿

本研究将I2V形式化为“初始视觉约束下的选择性状态转移”。首帧状态包括实体、属性、关系、姿态、背景和相机状态。

对每条样本，我把目标拆成两个集合。Transform集合描述必须发生什么变化，例如“左侧白狗由站立变为坐下”；Preserve集合描述哪些内容不应被破坏，例如棕狗身份、背景和相机构图保持稳定。

基于这个表示，当前设计了五个评测维度：属性绑定、动作绑定、运动绑定、背景动态和视角变换。每条样本进入Benchmark前还必须满足四项条件：目标可识别、变化可观察、主要维度可隔离、任务不是静态复制就能完成的平凡任务。

这一框架的价值在于支持更明确的失败归因。例如模型可能没有执行变化，可能把变化施加到错误主体，也可能通过相机平移制造主体移动的表面效果。最终评分从指令执行、非目标保持和过程连贯三个互补角度展开。

### English Script

I formalize I2V as a selective state transition under visual constraints. The initial frame defines entities, attributes, relations, poses, the background, and the camera state.

Each benchmark item is divided into two sets. The Transform set states what must change—for example, the white dog on the left should move from standing to sitting. The Preserve set states what should remain stable, such as the identity of the brown dog, the background, and the camera composition.

The current design covers five dimensions: attribute binding, action binding, motion binding, background dynamics, and view transformation. An item is admitted only when the target is identifiable, the change is observable, the main dimension can be isolated, and the task cannot be solved by copying the input frame.

This structure supports clearer failure attribution. A model may omit the requested change, apply it to the wrong subject, or use camera motion as a shortcut for object motion. The final evaluation therefore considers execution, preservation, and temporal coherence as three complementary aspects.

## Slide 5：Overall Pipeline Architecture（1分钟）

### 中文讲稿

整体技术路线分为四部分：先验提取、候选数据合成、质量控制和有效性验证。

三条设计原则贯穿流程。第一，语义配方必须能够追溯到真实用户数据，而不是任意拼接主体和动作；第二，每条样本只保留一个主要干预维度；第三，目标主体、目标变化和保持约束以结构化字段交付，下游评测器不应临时从自然语言中重新猜测。

截至本次中期检查，前两部分已经跑通候选生成；质量控制正在修复和回归审计；多模型有效性验证尚未完成。因此，后面展示的规模是候选数据规模，不是已经发布的最终Benchmark规模。

### English Script

The workflow contains four stages: prior extraction, candidate synthesis, quality control, and validity verification.

Three principles guide the design. First, each semantic recipe must be traceable to real user data rather than being formed by arbitrary combinations of subjects and actions. Second, each item should isolate one primary intervention. Third, target subjects, requested changes, and preservation constraints must be delivered as structured fields, so downstream evaluators do not have to recover them from free-form text.

At the midterm stage, candidate generation in the first two phases is operational. Quality repair and regression auditing are still in progress, and multi-model validity experiments have not yet been completed. The following counts therefore describe candidate assets, not a released benchmark.

## Slide 6：Phase 1 — Prior Data Preparation（1.25分钟）

### 中文讲稿

Phase 1以TIP-I2V真实用户提示词和图像数据作为先验来源。上层是五步解析流程：扫描和清洗原始数据；用VLM解析首帧中的主体、背景和相机状态；用LLM解析提示词中的语义意图；进行图文交叉验证；最后组装Prior Package。

下层包含六个确定性增强子模块，分别负责字段补齐、图文实例对齐、参考图抽取、频率分层、结构化配方生成和审计。这六个增强模块不再调用外部模型，因此可以幂等重跑；这里的“零API”只描述增强子模块，不代表Phase 1上层的VLM和LLM解析没有模型调用。

图文实例对齐目前采用完全匹配、子串匹配和未匹配三级规则。它的优点是可复现，但对同义词和复杂指代的处理能力有限。因此，对齐置信度将作为质量门控依据，低置信度样本需要人工复核或剔除。

### English Script

Phase 1 derives task priors from TIP-I2V, a large collection of real user image–prompt pairs. The upper pipeline has five steps: raw-data cleaning, visual parsing with a vision-language model, semantic intent parsing with a language model, cross-modal verification, and assembly of the Prior Package.

The lower pipeline contains six deterministic enhancement modules for field repair, image–text instance alignment, reference-image extraction, frequency analysis, recipe construction, and auditing. These modules make no additional API calls and can be rerun with the same result. This zero-API property applies only to the enhancement modules; the upper parsing pipeline still uses VLM and LLM inference.

The current instance matcher uses exact, substring, and unmatched levels. This design is reproducible, but it is limited when synonyms or complex references are involved. Alignment confidence is therefore treated as a quality signal, and low-confidence cases must be reviewed or removed.

## Slide 7：Phase 2 — Dataset Synthesis（1.5分钟）

### 中文讲稿

Phase 2通过八个步骤生成候选数据：配额规划、配方采样、题目计划、输入构造、VQA检查、提示词定稿、数据导出和全量审计。

这里有几项已经实现的工程设计。配额分配使用最大余数法，使整数子配额之和严格等于目标总量；每个语义配方保留TIP-I2V来源，便于追溯；图像同时保留原生宽高比版本和16比9推理伴生版本；VQA用于候选输入检查；提示词经过VLM描述、LLM润色和规则审计。

此外还设计了静态复制、随机运动、全局滤镜、相机平移替代和主体替换五类对照，用于检查评测器是否走捷径。

需要强调的是，流程“能够生成文件”不等于输出已经满足正式Schema。后续P0问题正是在跨阶段结构字段上发现的，因此结构审计必须成为发布前硬门控。

### English Script

Phase 2 constructs benchmark candidates through eight steps: quota allocation, recipe sampling, question planning, input construction, VQA-based checking, prompt finalization, export, and full auditing.

Several design choices are already implemented. The largest remainder method ensures that integer sub-quotas add up exactly to the required total. Every semantic recipe retains its TIP-I2V source for traceability. Each image has both a native-aspect-ratio version and a 16-by-9 inference companion. Candidate inputs are checked through structured VQA, and prompts pass through visual description, language polishing, and a final rule audit.

I also designed five contrastive controls: static copying, random motion, a global filter, camera-pan substitution, and subject swapping. These controls test whether an evaluator relies on shortcuts instead of the intended capability.

Most importantly, successful file generation does not guarantee compliance with the formal schema. The P0 issue was discovered in structured fields passed between stages. Structural completeness must therefore become a hard release gate.

## Slide 8：Data Funnel & Current Status（1分钟）

### 中文讲稿

这一页用数据漏斗统一四个容易混淆的数字。

最初有4,092条Question Plan，随后生成3,519条提示词候选，其中3,517条进入五维Candidate Manifest。3,517除以4,092等于85.9%，它表示进入候选manifest的比例，不是最终质量通过率。图像资产共8,184个，包括4,092张原生首帧和4,092张16比9伴生图；117条提示词使用fallback，占3,519条的3.3%。

正式目标规模是五个维度各300条，共1,500条。但当前仓库中的1,500条目录属于初步抽样包，还没有通过最终结构审计，不能称为已发布数据集。

### English Script

This slide separates four counts that are easy to confuse. The pipeline starts with 4,092 question plans and produces 3,519 prompt candidates. Of these, 3,517 enter the five-dimension candidate manifest. The ratio of 85.9 percent describes candidate-manifest coverage; it is not a final quality pass rate.

The asset collection contains 8,184 images: 4,092 native initial frames and the same number of 16-by-9 companions. Fallback prompts are used in 117 of the 3,519 cases, or 3.3 percent.

The planned release contains 1,500 samples—300 for each dimension. The current 1,500-sample folder is only a preliminary package. It has not passed the final structural audit and should not be presented as a released dataset.

## Slide 9：Quality Issues — P0 Critical Finding（1.5分钟）

### 中文讲稿

最关键的问题是P0结构化目标失效。这里必须区分修复前基线和当前状态。

在冻结的旧版基线中，3,517条候选的目标主体noun和结构化目标变化全部缺失，主体描述统一退化为“the subject”。根因包括Phase 1和Phase 2字段名不一致、五维槽位没有被正确消费、v2文件发现不稳定，以及导出阶段缺少足够严格的硬门控。

当前工作区已经完成部分修复：3,517条中有3,263条恢复了主体noun，覆盖率为92.8%；但仍有254条为空或使用泛化主体。更重要的是，3,517条`target_relation`目前仍为空。因此P0还没有闭环，当前manifest和由它抽取的初步1,500条数据包都不能直接交给Phase 3。

下一步顺序是：先重建结构化关系，再处理或剔除254条残余主体，随后重跑导出硬门控和全量审计，最后冻结版本、哈希和数据卡。只有这些条件同时满足，才能形成正式发布候选。

### English Script

The most serious finding is a P0 failure in the structured evaluation targets. It is important to separate the frozen repair baseline from the current workspace.

In the old baseline, all 3,517 candidates were missing both the target-subject noun and the structured target change. Subject descriptions had collapsed to the generic phrase “the subject.” The main causes were mismatched field names between phases, unused dimension-specific slots, unstable discovery of versioned files, and weak export gates.

The current repair has recovered subject nouns for 3,263 samples, which gives 92.8 percent coverage. However, 254 cases still contain an empty or generic subject. More importantly, `target_relation` remains empty for all 3,517 rows. P0 is therefore not closed, and neither the manifest nor the preliminary 1,500-sample package is ready for Phase 3.

The next steps are to reconstruct the structured relations, resolve or reject the remaining subject cases, rerun all hard gates and audits, and then freeze the version, hashes, and dataset card. Only after these checks can the package become a release candidate.

## Slide 10：Quality Issues — P1 to P4（0.75分钟）

### 中文讲稿

除P0外，还有四类输入质量问题。

P1是5.8%的提示词命中低频词表，但这些词不能一律删除：必要实体或属性术语应该保留，只有可替换或无关的文学化修饰需要简化。P2是部分源图分辨率较低，直接放大会模糊，但生成式超分辨率也可能引入身份和纹理幻觉。P3是原生宽高比范围较大，统一输入时可能裁掉目标或引入黑边。P4是主体频率与难度分布尚未完成校准。

这些问题的共同原则是：质量修复不能改变题目本身的语义真值。

### English Script

Four additional input-quality issues remain. First, 5.8 percent of prompts match the low-frequency word list. Necessary entity or attribute terms should be retained, while replaceable literary modifiers can be simplified. Second, some source images have low resolution. Simple resizing creates blur, but generative super-resolution may introduce false identity or texture details. Third, the native aspect ratios vary widely, so normalization may crop the target or add padding. Fourth, subject frequency and difficulty have not yet been calibrated.

The shared rule is that quality processing must not change the semantic ground truth of an evaluation item.

## Slide 11：Future Work — Repair & Validation（1.5分钟）

### 中文讲稿

下一阶段包含三条主线。

第一条是P0闭环和版本管理：补齐`target_relation`，处理剩余254条主体，建立导出硬门控，并在重新打包前冻结manifest版本。

第二条是比较质量处理策略。文本侧把必要术语、可简化修饰词和无关表达分开处理；图像侧比较插值、非生成式锐化和生成式超分辨率。生成式增强只有在身份和属性保持检查通过后才能使用。宽高比适配方式和有效区域也要写入元数据。

第三条是消融和有效性验证。每个维度抽取50到100条，选择2到3个明确支持“图像加文本”条件的I2V模型，冻结模型版本和推理配置。自动评分使用目标检测、光流和特征匹配等代理指标，同时对部分样本进行盲法人工标注，检验自动指标与人工判断的一致性。

实验检验的是维度区分性、难度区分性和失败模式标签的有效性，而不是预先证明某个模型一定在哪个维度更好。

### English Script

The next stage has three workstreams. The first closes P0 and establishes version control: reconstruct `target_relation`, resolve the remaining 254 subjects, add hard export gates, and freeze a new manifest before packaging.

The second compares quality-processing strategies. On the text side, necessary technical terms will be separated from optional or irrelevant modifiers. On the image side, I will compare interpolation, non-generative sharpening, and generative super-resolution. A generative enhancement will be accepted only if identity and attribute preservation are verified. Aspect-ratio adaptation and the valid image region will also be recorded in metadata.

The third workstream covers ablation and validity experiments. I will sample 50 to 100 items per dimension and evaluate two or three I2V models that support both image and text conditions, with fixed model versions and inference settings. Automatic proxy metrics—such as object detection, optical flow, and feature similarity—will be calibrated against blind human ratings.

The goal is to test dimension separation, difficulty ordering, and the usefulness of failure-mode labels. The experiments will not assume in advance that a particular model must perform better on any dimension.

## Slide 13：Conclusion & Interim Contributions（0.75分钟）

### 中文讲稿

总结来说，目前形成了四项阶段性成果。

第一，提出Preserve–Transform双轴表示，把目标变化和非目标保持组织到同一评测任务中。第二，实现了可追溯到真实用户数据的候选构建流水线。第三，形成3,519条提示词候选和8,184个图像资产，正式发布目标为五维各300条。第四，建立了覆盖结构字段、提示词、图像、宽高比和分布的P0到P4审计框架。

当前最重要的未完成边界是：P0仍未闭环，1,500条初步数据包还不是正式发布版本。下一阶段将以完成结构修复、质量策略比较和多模型有效性验证为重点，最终形成可复现、可审计的Benchmark发布候选。

我的汇报到这里，谢谢各位老师，欢迎批评指正。

### English Script

To conclude, the project has produced four interim outputs. First, the Preserve–Transform framework represents requested change and required preservation within one evaluation task.
