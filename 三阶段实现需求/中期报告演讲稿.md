## Slide 2: Research Motivation (2 min)

Let me start with the motivation. Image-to-Video generation has seen remarkable progress in recent years, with models like Stable Video Diffusion, CogVideoX, and DynamiCrafter producing increasingly realistic results. However, visual quality alone does not tell the full story.

首先介绍研究动机。近年来，图像到视频生成技术取得了显著进展，Stable Video Diffusion、CogVideoX、DynamiCrafter等模型生成的视频越来越逼真。然而，视觉质量本身并不能说明全部问题。

I want to briefly share how this project evolved. Initially, our research targeted **long video generation benchmarks** — evaluating models that produce videos of one to five minutes. However, through systematic literature review, we discovered a fundamental insight: all current long video generation methods — whether chunked synthesis like StreamingT2V, autoregressive approaches like LongLive, or hierarchical methods like NUWA-XL — essentially follow the same underlying paradigm. They decompose a long video into short segments, use the **last frame of one segment as the conditioning first frame for the next**, and perform I2V generation segment by segment before stitching them together. In other words, long video generation is fundamentally **a chain of I2V tasks**. Moreover, existing evaluation metrics such as FVD and CLIPScore are designed for short clips of 16 to 64 frames and cannot effectively assess long video quality — when quality degrades, it is impossible to attribute the failure to single-segment I2V deficiency, stitching strategy flaws, or global planning errors. This realization led us to a critical pivot: rather than evaluating the entire long video pipeline where stitching strategies obscure fundamental model capabilities, we focus on the atomic building block — single-segment I2V generation — which offers higher diagnostic precision, full reproducibility, significantly lower computational cost, and crucially, the feasibility of deploying automated evaluation tools such as Grounding DINO and optical flow estimation for precise diagnosis.

我想简要分享这个项目的演进过程。最初，我们的研究目标是**长视频生成基准**——评测生成1到5分钟视频的模型。然而，通过系统的文献调研，我们发现了一个基本洞察：当前所有长视频生成方法——无论是StreamingT2V这样的分段拼接式、LongLive这样的自回归式，还是NUWA-XL这样的层次化方法——本质上都遵循相同的底层范式。它们将长视频分解为短片段，以**前一段的末帧作为下一段的条件首帧**，逐段执行I2V生成后拼接。换言之，长视频生成本质上是**一系列I2V任务的链接**。更重要的是，现有评测指标如FVD、CLIPScore等针对16–64帧短片段设计，无法有效评估长视频质量——当质量下降时，无法将问题归因于单段I2V能力不足、拼接策略缺陷或全局规划失误。这一认识促使我们进行了关键转向：与其评测整个长视频流水线（拼接策略会掩盖模型的基础能力），不如聚焦原子构建块——单段I2V生成——它提供更高的诊断精度、完全的可复现性、显著更低的计算成本，更关键的是可部署Grounding DINO、光流估计等自动化评测工具进行精确诊断。

Consider this scenario: a user provides an image of two dogs — one white and one brown — with the instruction "make the white dog sit down." A model might produce a smooth, high-quality video where *the brown dog* sits instead, or where both dogs simply stand still with a subtle camera zoom. Traditional metrics like FVD or CLIPScore would rate these outputs favorably, yet the user's intent was clearly violated.

设想这样一个场景：用户提供了一张包含白色和棕色两只狗的图像，指令是"让白色的狗坐下"。模型可能生成一段流畅的高质量视频，但坐下的是棕色的狗，或者两只狗都静止不动、仅有轻微的相机缩放。传统指标如FVD或CLIPScore会给出较高评分，但用户意图显然被违反了。

This reveals a critical gap: existing benchmarks lack the ability to diagnose **compositional failures** — situations where the model fails to bind actions, attributes, or motions to the correct subjects under the constraints of a given input image.

这揭示了一个关键缺口：现有基准缺乏诊断**组合性失败**的能力——即模型在给定输入图像约束下，未能将动作、属性或运动正确绑定到指定主体的情况。

## Slide 3: Gap in Existing Benchmarks (1 min)

To put our work in context, let me briefly compare with existing evaluation benchmarks. T2I-CompBench evaluates compositional generation in the text-to-image domain but does not handle temporal dynamics. T2V-CompBench extends this to video but assumes text-only input without first-frame constraints. VBench and VBench++ provide comprehensive video quality metrics but lack fine-grained diagnosis for compositional correctness.

为了定位我们的工作，简要对比现有评测基准。T2I-CompBench评估文本到图像的组合生成，但不涉及时间动态。T2V-CompBench将其扩展到视频，但假设纯文本输入，没有首帧约束。VBench和VBench++提供了全面的视频质量指标，但缺乏对组合正确性的细粒度诊断。

Our key insight is that I2V generation introduces a unique **dual constraint**: the input image defines an inviolable initial state — subject identities, spatial layout, background — while the text instruction demands **selective changes** to only specific elements. No existing benchmark explicitly models this Preserve-Transform duality.

我们的核心洞察是，I2V生成引入了独特的**双重约束**：输入图像定义了不可违背的初始状态——主体身份、空间布局、背景——而文本指令要求仅对特定元素进行**选择性变化**。现有基准均未显式建模这种Preserve-Transform对偶性。

## Slide 4: Preserve-Transform Framework (2 min)

Now let me introduce our evaluation framework. We formalize I2V generation as **selective state transition under initial visual constraints**. Given an input image I₀ and a text prompt p, the initial visual state encompasses entities, attributes, relations, poses, background, and camera state.

接下来介绍我们的评测框架。我们将I2V生成形式化为**初始视觉约束下的选择性状态转移**。给定输入图像I₀和文本提示p，初始视觉状态涵盖实体、属性、关系、姿态、背景和相机状态。

Each evaluation sample is decomposed into two sets: a **Transform set** specifying what must change, and a **Preserve set** specifying what must remain stable. This dual-axis decomposition enables precise failure attribution — we can distinguish whether a model failed to execute the intended change, or whether it unintentionally modified non-target elements.

每条评测样本被分解为两个集合：**Transform集合**指定必须发生什么变化，**Preserve集合**指定什么必须保持稳定。这种双轴分解实现了精确的失败归因——我们可以区分模型是未能执行目标变化，还是意外修改了非目标元素。

Based on this framework, we define five evaluation dimensions tailored to I2V: **Attribute Binding** — whether attribute changes are applied to the correct subject; **Action Binding** — whether the specified subject performs the intended action; **Motion Binding** — whether the subject moves along the correct trajectory; **Background Dynamics** — whether environmental changes occur without corrupting foreground subjects; and **View Transformation** — whether camera movements are executed without faking them through subject displacement.

基于此框架，我们定义了五个面向I2V的评测维度：**属性绑定**——属性变化是否施加于正确主体；**动作绑定**——指定主体是否执行了目标动作；**运动绑定**——主体是否沿正确轨迹运动；**背景动态**——环境变化是否发生且不破坏前景主体；**视角变换**——相机运动是否真实执行而非通过主体位移伪造。

We also establish a validity condition: a sample enters the benchmark only when the target is identifiable, the change is observable, dimensions are separable, and the task is non-trivial.

我们还建立了有效性条件：样本进入基准的前提是目标可识别、变化可观察、维度可隔离、任务非平凡。

## Slide 5: Overall Pipeline Architecture (1 min)

Moving on to our technical approach. The overall pipeline follows a four-stage architecture: Prior Extraction, Automated Synthesis, Quality Control, and Validity Verification.

接下来介绍技术路线。整体流水线遵循四阶段架构：先验提取、自动化合成、质量控制和有效性验证。

Three design principles govern the entire system. First, a **zero-assembly vocabulary** principle — all test items must be sampled from real user data priors, with no heuristic composition allowed. Second, **dimension isolation** — each sample contains exactly one primary intervention dimension. Third, **structured delivery** — downstream evaluators consume structured fields directly without requiring NLU parsing.

三条设计原则贯穿整个系统。第一，**零拼词表原则**——所有题目必须从真实用户数据先验中采样，禁止启发式拼接。第二，**维度隔离**——每条样本仅包含一个主要干预维度。第三，**结构化交付**——下游评测器直接消费结构化字段，无需NLU反向解析。

## Slide 6: Phase 1 — Prior Data Preparation (2 min)

Phase 1 takes the TIP-I2V dataset — a million-scale real user prompt-image dataset — as our prior source. This is crucial: rather than generating synthetic prompts, we ground our benchmark in actual user behavior.

Phase 1以TIP-I2V数据集——一个百万级真实用户提示-图像数据集——作为先验来源。这一点至关重要：我们不是生成合成提示词，而是将基准扎根于真实用户行为。

The Phase 1 pipeline consists of a five-step parsing module plus six enhancement sub-modules. The five steps include raw data scanning, VLM-based visual structure parsing of first frames, LLM-based semantic intent parsing of prompts, cross-modal alignment verification, and Prior Package assembly.

Phase 1流水线由五步解析模块加六个增强子模块组成。五个步骤包括：原始数据扫描、基于VLM的首帧视觉结构解析、基于LLM的提示词语义意图解析、跨模态对齐验证和先验包组装。

The six enhancement sub-modules operate under a strict constraint: zero API calls, purely deterministic rules, and idempotent re-runability. These include field patching, image-text instance alignment using a greedy matching algorithm, reference image bank extraction, frequency-tier stratification into head, torso, and long-tail categories, structured recipe generation, and comprehensive auditing.

六个增强子模块在严格约束下运行：零API调用、纯确定性规则、幂等可重跑。包括字段补齐、基于贪心匹配算法的图文实例对齐、参考图库抽取、head/torso/long_tail频率分层、结构化配方生成和全面审计。

## Slide 7: Phase 2 — Dataset Synthesis (2 min)

Phase 2 receives the prior output and produces the final evaluation dataset through an eight-step pipeline. Let me highlight the key innovations.

Phase 2接收先验输出，通过八步流水线产出最终评测数据集。让我重点介绍几个关键创新。

The **Largest Remainder Method** for quota allocation ensures that integer quotas are distributed proportionally without rounding errors — the sum of all sub-quotas exactly equals the target total. **Recipe sampling** enforces the zero-assembly principle: every test item traces back to a TIP-I2V source video. The **dual-track image output** produces both native aspect-ratio first frames and 16:9 inference companions for different model input requirements.

配额分配采用**最大余数法**，确保整数配额按比例精确分配、无四舍五入误差——所有子配额之和精确等于目标总量。**配方采样**强制执行零拼词表原则：每条测试项可追溯至TIP-I2V源视频。**双轨图像输出**同时产出原始宽高比首帧和16:9推理伴生件，以适配不同模型输入要求。

For quality control, we implement VQA-based structured verification with three-state aggregation. For prompt finalization, a VLM-describe-then-LLM-polish pipeline ensures fluency while maintaining dimension-specific forbidden word constraints.

在质量控制方面，我们实现了基于VQA的结构化验证与三态聚合。在提示词定稿方面，VLM描述+LLM润色的流水线确保流畅性，同时维持维度特定的禁词约束。

We also introduce **five types of contrastive controls** — static copy, random motion, global filter, camera pan cheat, and subject swap — designed for anti-shortcut validation.

我们还引入了**五种对照设计**——静态复制、随机运动、全局滤镜、相机平移作弊、主体替换——用于反捷径验证。

## Slide 8: Current Scale & Output (0.5 min)

In terms of scale, our current dataset contains **3,519 prompts** and **8,184 image assets** — comprising 4,092 native first frames plus 4,092 corresponding 16:9 inference companions. The VQA pass rate stands at 85.9%, with only 3.3% using fallback prompts. Each sample is delivered as a 17-field flat BenchmarkSample schema, directly consumable by Phase 3 evaluators.

在规模方面，当前数据集包含**3,519条提示词**和**8,184个图像资产**——由4,092张原生首帧加4,092张对应的16:9推理伴生件组成。VQA通过率为85.9%，仅3.3%使用了回退提示词。每条样本以17字段扁平BenchmarkSample Schema交付，可直接被Phase 3评测器消费。

## Slide 9: Quality Issues — P0 Critical Finding (1.5 min)

Now, let me discuss the quality issues we discovered through systematic auditing. This is an important contribution because most benchmark papers do not discuss data quality control at all.

接下来讨论我们通过系统审计发现的质量问题。这是一项重要贡献，因为大多数基准论文完全不讨论数据质量控制。

The most critical finding is **P0: Structured Evaluation Targets Completely Empty**. After full-schema auditing of all 3,517 candidates, we found that every single entry has empty target_subjects noun fields, degraded subject descriptions showing only generic placeholders like "the subject," and missing structured target relations. This means Phase 3 evaluators cannot consume the current manifest at all.

最关键的发现是**P0：结构化评测目标完全为空**。对全部3,517条候选进行全量Schema审计后，我们发现每一条的target_subjects noun字段均为空、主体描述退化为"the subject"等泛化占位符、结构化目标关系缺失。这意味着Phase 3评测器完全无法消费当前manifest。

Root cause analysis revealed five contributing factors: field name inconsistency between Phase 1 and Phase 2, unused dimension-specific slot fields, incorrect file name resolution for v2 outputs, natural language polish masking structural emptiness, and inverted constraint logic for View Transformation. Importantly, the candidate pool audit confirms sufficient samples exist after excluding P0 issues — the bottleneck is code-level bugs, not data scarcity.

根因分析揭示了五个贡献因素：Phase 1与Phase 2之间的字段名不一致、维度特定槽位字段未被消费、v2输出的文件名解析错误、自然语言润色掩盖结构化空缺、以及View Transformation的约束逻辑反转。重要的是，候选池审计确认排除P0问题后仍有充足样本——瓶颈是代码层面的bug，而非数据不足。

## Slide 10: Quality Issues — P1 to P4 (1 min)

Beyond P0, we identified four additional quality concerns. **P1**: 5.8% of prompts contain rare vocabulary such as "malevolence" or "sclera" — these test text encoder robustness rather than compositional ability. **P2**: Source images from TIP-I2V are typically 224×126 resolution; enlarging them 3.8× to 854×480 produces severe blurring with only 6.9% information density. **P3**: Native aspect ratios range from 0.53 to 2.52, with only 2% near standard ratios, creating inconsistency for evaluation. **P4**: Subject frequency distribution and difficulty calibration are uncontrolled.

除P0外，我们还发现了四类质量问题。**P1**：5.8%的提示词含生僻词如"malevolence"或"sclera"——测试的是文本编码器鲁棒性而非组合能力。**P2**：TIP-I2V源图通常为224×126分辨率，3.8倍放大至854×480后严重模糊，信息密度仅6.9%。**P3**：原生宽高比范围0.53~2.52，仅2%接近标准比例，导致评测不一致。**P4**：主体频率分布和难度标定未受控。

## Slide 11: Future Work — Repair & Validation (2 min)

For the next stage, our plan consists of three main threads. First, **systematic repair**: P0 requires six targeted code fixes followed by a full pipeline re-run. For P1 through P4, we will implement a three-layer rare word defense with Zipf frequency thresholds, Real-ESRGAN plus GFPGAN for image enhancement, intelligent aspect-ratio adaptation, and frequency-tier-aware sampling.

下一阶段计划包含三条主线。第一，**系统性修复**：P0需要六项定向代码修复后全量重跑流水线。P1至P4将实施三层生僻词防护（Zipf频率阈值）、Real-ESRGAN加GFPGAN图像增强、智能宽高比适配和频率分层采样。

Second, **ablation experiments**: we will measure the independent contribution of each repair through controlled comparisons — rare word prompts versus simplified prompts measured by CLIP similarity and inter-model variance, LANCZOS versus Real-ESRGAN measured by Laplacian Variance and FID, mixed ratios versus unified 854×480 measured by evaluator stability.

第二，**消融实验**：通过对照比较测量每项修复的独立贡献——含生僻词与简化提示词对比CLIP相似度和模型间方差、LANCZOS与Real-ESRGAN对比Laplacian Variance和FID、混合宽高比与统一854×480对比评测器稳定性。

Third, **validity verification**: we will sample 50-100 items per dimension, run 2-3 representative I2V models, and verify three propositions — dimension discriminability, difficulty discriminability, and failure mode predictability.

第三，**有效性验证**：每维度抽样50-100条，运行2-3个代表性I2V模型，验证三个命题——维度区分性、难度区分性和失败模式可预测性。

## Slide 13: Conclusion & Contributions (1 min)

To summarize, this work makes four main contributions. First, we propose the **Preserve-Transform dual-axis framework** that formally decomposes I2V evaluation into what must change and what must remain. Second, we develop a **fully automated, prior-grounded synthesis pipeline** that produces benchmark samples traceable to real user data. Third, we achieve a dataset scale of **3,519 prompts with 8,184 image assets** across five compositional dimensions. Fourth, we establish a **systematic data quality methodology** — identifying and categorizing P0 through P4 issues that, to our knowledge, have not been discussed in prior benchmark construction work.

总结而言，本工作有四项主要贡献。第一，提出**Preserve-Transform双轴框架**，将I2V评测形式化分解为必须变化和必须保持两部分。第二，开发了**全自动、先验驱动的合成流水线**，产出可追溯至真实用户数据的基准样本。第三，达到**3,519条提示词、8,184个图像资产**的数据集规模，覆盖五个组合维度。第四，建立了**系统化的数据质量方法论**——识别和分类P0至P4问题，据我们所知这在先前基准构建工作中未被讨论过。

The next phase will focus on completing all repairs, running ablation studies, and producing a validated, publication-ready benchmark dataset.

下一阶段将聚焦于完成所有修复、运行消融研究，并产出经验证的、可发表质量的基准数据集。

谢谢。欢迎各位老师提问。
